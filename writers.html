Ronan Mc Guire

Co-founder, co-editor

Ronan has worked in startups in Dublin, Berlin, and San Francisco, and has always maintained an interest in society and politics. Ronan and Louise came together to found SoWhat out of a dissatisfaction with current news sources, and a desire to critically understand the world.

Sydney Weinberg

Sydney Weinberg is a writer and environmental activist based in Dublin. Her writing has appeared in Long Story, Short, Banshee, The Dublin Review and the short fiction anthology Young Irelanders. She participated in 2016's Break Free From Fossil Fuels movement by helping to shut down the UK's largest opencast coal mine, and she works part-time for the Sustainable Water Network.

So What

So What is a collection of writers that analyse Irish and world events. We dig deep and offer context to what is going on in the world.

Newsletters are sent via email once per week, with the archived stories availble online several days later.
